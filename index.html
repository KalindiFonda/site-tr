SCREENING NOW

The 18-year-old Andrej is sent to a youth detention centre due to problematic behaviour.
There, he meets Željko, the informal leader of the detainees. Upon discovering Andrej’s secret, Željko soon begins to take advantage of him; Andrej’s sense of responsibility and moral integrity are put to a trial. Ultimately, he must choose between Željko and his reckless lifestyle and staying true to himself.
